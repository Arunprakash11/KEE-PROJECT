Core Business Activities

We specialize in the supply and servicing of Mechanical and Control & Instrumentation (C&I) spares for thermal power plants. Our offerings include:

1. Boiler Control & Instrumentation Spares

  • Drum steam/water level indicator : Ascertor, Display Driver, Relay Driver, Power Supply, Motherboards
  • Flame Scanners : Head Assembly, Flame Processor (2/4 module), Power Supply, System Motherboard
  • High Energy Arc Ignitor : Spark Tip, Excitor Box, Flexible Cable, Spark Rod, Retractor Assembly
  • Steam Leak Detection : ASLD Sensor, Field Amplifier, Signal Processor, Alarm Processor, Audio Channel Selector
  • Coal Feeder System : Signal Interface, Conditioning, Motor Control, Relay Interface, Pulse Relay Module, Communication Interface, Relays, Timers, Chokes

2. On-site Calibration

  • Flame Scanners
  • Coal Feeder
  • Steam Leak Detection System

3. Mechanical Spares

  • Gravimetric Feeder Spares
  • Coal Feeder Inlet/Outlet Gates
  • Oil Gun Assembly
  • Scanner & Igniter Guide Pipes
